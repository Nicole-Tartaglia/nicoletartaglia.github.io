BIOGRAPHY

I am an artist, designer, and developer. I work fulltime as a UI/X and Visual designer for a large private company. I have an arts specific brand, nicoletartaglia.com, which produces a wide variety of design and art projects including fine art production, digital and large-scale paintings, art curation, and more. The Nodding Pansy is a new project that I am orking on with my husband which is off-the-grid living quarters on a seculded lot in Upper Black Eddy, PA featuing a geodesic dome. I graduated from Rutgers University in 2005, receiving a Bachelor of the Arts degree in Graphic Design and Computer Science. I served on the AIGA Philadelphia board as Interactive Director for 3 years (7 years including commitee volunteer work before graduating to Interactive Director) curating an all QR code animation show and setting up many web workshops for the community. My work has been exhibited in many galleries and events nationally. I live in New Hope, PA with my husband, three cats, daughter, Colette and son, Basil.

Web Specific

I am currently a Senior Web Usability and Designer for Edmund Optics® servicing there website. The website is a full service e-commerce site with 34,000 products available for purchase and is translated in over 14 languages. Additionally, it provides over 1,000 pieces of content for free for customers. Main expertise includes Data Analysis, Mock-ups, Prototypes, CSS, Javascript, Responsive design, etc. Until 2020, I was the only web usability and designer for the entire website. In 2020, the team expanded and management of a small team became an additional responsibility of mine. The creative team at Edmund Optics® includese a videographer, a photographer, and print designers whose work rolls over to the web. Additionally, there are several data analysts as well as a full service backend programmer department in which I collaborate with daily.

I have worked at Edmund Optics® for 16 years moving through the company with a promotion apx. every three years or so. The company has grown significantly throughout the years so I was fortuante to grow with the company as well. Starting as a Production Artist, working on their print catalog, other titles are in order as follows: Graphic Designer, Web Designer, UI/UX Designer, Senior UI/UX Designer. I've also received numerous internal awards including Employee of the Month, Most Valuable Team Member (2), and was nomiated for the Innovation Award.

About Edmund Optics®: Edmund Optics® (EO) has been a leading global manufacturer and supplier of optics, imaging, and photonics technology that has served a variety of markets including Life Sciences, Biomedical, Industrial Inspection, Semiconductor, R&D, and Defense since 1942. EO designs and manufactures a wide selection of optical components, multi-element lenses, imaging systems, and optomechanical equipment, while supporting original equipment manufacturer (OEM) applications with volume production of stock and custom products. The company employs over 1,000 employees in more than nine countries across the globe and continues to expand.
